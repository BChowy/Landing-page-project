STORY

Oppenheimer is a 2023 historical drama film about J. Robert Oppenheimer, the scientist who led the Manhattan Project to develop the atomic bomb. The film is directed by Christopher Nolan and stars Cillian Murphy as Oppenheimer.

The film follows Oppenheimer from his early days as a student at Harvard University to his leadership of the Manhattan Project during World War II. It also explores the moral and ethical implications of the atomic bomb, and Oppenheimer's own personal struggles with the knowledge that he had helped to create such a destructive weapon.


"It's very easy to make the case for Oppenheimer as the most important person who ever lived, he is the individual who was able to marshal the forces effectively."

- Christopher Nolan
